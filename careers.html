Careers at Hot Beans Web

We are always looking for motivated people who want to build a career in web development. Our roles are designed to support trainees and junior developers while still giving them responsibility on real projects. Below are some of the typical positions we offer.

Junior Front‑End Developer

Skills: HTML, CSS, basic JavaScript, responsive design.

Description: Work with our design and development team to build user interfaces, fix layout issues and improve accessibility across client websites.

Junior Back‑End Developer

Skills: Basic knowledge of Python, PHP or Node.js, databases.

Description: Help maintain server‑side code, connect websites to databases and support the development of APIs for client projects.

Trainee Web Developer

Skills: Completed or studying a web development course, strong interest in coding.

Description: Rotate between front‑end and back‑end tasks, attend training sessions and work closely with mentors to build your skills.

If any of these roles sound interesting, visit our Apply page to submit your application. We welcome candidates from different backgrounds as long as they are willing to learn and work hard.
