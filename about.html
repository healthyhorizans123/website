About Hot Beans Web

Hot Beans Web was created to bridge the gap between education and real‑world web development. Many new developers finish college or online courses but struggle to gain practical experience. Our company focuses on giving trainees the chance to work on real projects with guidance from experienced developers, designers and project managers.

We believe in clean, accessible and responsive web design. Every website we build is designed to work across desktops, tablets and mobile phones. We also care about performance and search engine optimisation, so our clients can reach their audiences effectively. At the same time, we use each project as a learning opportunity for our trainees, helping them build strong portfolios and professional confidence.

Our culture is friendly and collaborative. Trainees are encouraged to ask questions, share ideas and experiment with new technologies. We regularly run internal workshops on topics such as JavaScript frameworks, version control, accessibility and UX design. By joining Hot Beans Web, you are not just getting a job—you are joining a learning community.
